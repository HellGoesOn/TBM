[c/ffff00:Terrarian's Bizzare Modventure] is a mod that aims to recreate abilities from JoJo's Bizzare Adventure as faithfully as possible, while providing a fresh way to fight in Terraria!
[c/00ff00:Current Features:]
-Stand Arrow which allows you acquire Stands.
-Stamina system, a resource that you require to use your Stand's abilities!

[c/0000ff:Currently Implemented Stands and their Abilities:]

Melee:
[c/000055:Star Platinum]
-Can stop time for 7 seconds(requires at least 100 stamina). During stopped time, you cannot gain any Stamina.
[c/222255:Sticky Fingers]
-Can unzip its hand to attack at higher range
